FAQ's

What time should I arrive for the ceremony?

We recommend you arrive at least 30 minutes before the ceremony. The Missing Bean (opposite Lincoln) serves good coffee.

What happens after the ceremony?
The Bride and Groom will take photos for an hour. There will be some group shots at Lincoln and then guests will be transported by bus to The Perch for welcome drinks and canapés.

Will the ceremony/recption be indoors or outdoors?

The ceremony will be indoors. The reception will be in a marquee in The Perch’s garden, which has lovely outdoor spaces.

Will food and drink be served at the Reception?

Yes. Welcome drinks and canapés on arrival from 3-4:30pm, followed by the Wedding breakfast with sharing platters, wine and fizz. In the evening, food will be provided for both evening and day guests, with fizz for evening guests on arrival. The marquee has a bar where guests can purchase more drinks during the evening.

Will there be vegan/vegetarian/gluten free/special diet options?

We can accommodate dietary requirements - just let us know when you RSVP.

What happens after the wedding breakfast?

There will be a 45 minute break while the Perch sets up the dance floor. We plan to have some games outside! Evening guests will arrive from 8:30pm, ready for dancing!

What happens at the end of the night?

We have to turn music off at 11:30pm and leave The Perch by 12am. There are taxis available for booking from The Perch to help you get back to your accommodation safely!

When do I need to RSVP by?

Please let us know online if you can make it by 25th April 2022!

Can I take photos during the wedding?

We ask that you don’t take pictures during the ceremony, as we have a photographer on hand. After that, we would love you to take pictures and share them with us afterwards!

Is there a dress code?

Formal or black tie optional. Dress to impress!

Are children welcome?

We hope you will understand our decision to make the wedding child-free, and take the opportunity to let your hair down and celebrate in style!

I have other questions! Who can I contact?

Ask Becky! (or Jack at a pinch)
